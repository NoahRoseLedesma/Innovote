With October upon us we're reminded of the onset of autumn by the smattering of fall colors lining our streets, the yellows and oranges add to the greens we've become accustomed to. Though the greens are a different now, not sectioned and organized side by side on their branches as they've been in the past, they too are now found scattered on sidewalks, upon the grass, and in Puget Sound. 

The new age of bike share in Seattle has lead to marketing battle between three companies all looking to fill the space vacated by Pronto. It has also lead to an increase in undocked bikes littering the streets and to a renewed conversation about helmet laws in our city.

Prompt
You have been hired by the city to deal with the "helmet issue." Provide a solution, whether it be a new helmet, system, or policy that will lead to fewer cyclist breaking the law while preserving the safety of our velo favoring friends. Be original and creative but also look at what works in other places around the world while considering the needs and conditions of Seattle cyclists.
